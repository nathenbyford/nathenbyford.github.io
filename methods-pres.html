Introduction

The data for this analysis consists of data from 5 texas counties of oberservations of childhood bmi. These counties are; Bastrop, Cameron, Dimmit, Hidalgo, and Mclennan Counties. The data provides information about the childs age, gender, height, weight, bmi, county, and when the observation was taken.
